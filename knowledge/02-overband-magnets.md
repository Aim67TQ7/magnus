# Overband Magnetic Separator Technical Reference

## Overview

Overband (cross-belt) magnetic separators are suspended above conveyor belts to extract tramp ferrous metal from bulk material streams. They are the primary equipment protection solution for crushers, shredders, mills, and downstream processing equipment. Available in permanent and electromagnetic configurations.

## Selection Decision Framework

### Choose PCB (Permanent) When:
- Mobile or portable installations (no power available)
- Light to moderate tramp loads
- Suspension heights ≤16" (400mm)
- Budget-conscious applications
- Construction/demolition recycling

### Choose EMAX (Air-Cooled) When:
- Challenging metal removal requiring higher field strength
- Suspension heights 16-20" (400-500mm)
- Need 185% strength over conventional electromagnets
- Power-to-weight ratio is critical
- Non-ATEX environments

### Choose OCW (Oil-Cooled) When:
- Maximum separation strength required
- Deep burden depths requiring deep field penetration
- Suspension heights up to 32" (800mm)
- Belt widths up to 96" (2400mm)
- ATEX-rated environments
- Heavy mining/aggregate applications
- 24/7 continuous duty

## Design Calculations

### Force Factor
```
Force Factor = (Belt Width × Core Dimension) / 10
```

### Critical Rules
- **Burden >10"** → Must use electromagnet (permanent cannot penetrate)
- **Aggregate/ore** → Default to electromagnet for deep reach
- **Tire recycling/turf** → Specify armored/urethane belt (wire puncture prevention)
- **Support structure** → Non-ferrous (300-series SS) within 18-24" of magnet
- **Steel structure warning** → Will magnetize, creating operational hazards
- **Magnet width** → Within ±20% of conveyor belt width

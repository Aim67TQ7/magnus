## Magnetic Property Classification

| Category | Materials | Magnet Required | Example Equipment |
|----------|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Highly Magnetic** | Carbon steel, cast iron, mild steel | Standard ceramic adequate for large pieces; rare earth for small | Plate, overband, drum, grate |
| **Moderately Magnetic** | 400-series stainless, some tool steels | Rare earth (high intensity) | HF drawer, high-intensity drum |
| **Weakly Magnetic** | 300-series stainless (work-hardened), some alloys | Ultra-high intensity (HISC, high gradient) | HISC, high-gradient separator |
| **Non-Ferrous** | Aluminum, copper, brass, zinc, lead | Eddy current separator OR metal detector | ECS, metal detector |
| **Non-Magnetic** | Plastics, glass, rubber, wood, stone | Metal detector only | METRON series detectors |

## Equipment Selection by Tramp Size

| Tramp Size Category | Example Items | Minimum Equipment |
|--------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Large (>6")** | Roof bolts, drill steel, crusher wear | Standard ceramic overband or plate |
| **Medium (1-6")** | Bolts, chain links, bucket teeth | Standard to high-intensity, depending on burial depth |
| **Small (1/4"-1")** | Hardware, knife chips, bearing fragments | Rare earth (10,000+ gauss) |
| **Fine (<1/4")** | Wire fragments, scale, weld spatter, dust | High-intensity rare earth + metal detector verification |
| **Stainless (any size)** | Equipment wear, trim, springs | HISC / ultra-high intensity + metal detector |
| **Non-ferrous (any size)** | Aluminum, copper, brass | Eddy current separator + metal detector |
